Badminton

  1. Badminton is a racket sport for two or four people.
  2. There is a net in the middle of the playgroud.
  3. There are two sides on the sides of the net, players should stay in their own part.
  4. Each side may only strike the shuttlecock once before it passes over the net.
  5. The game developed in British India from the eaelier game of battledore and shuttlecock.
